ACCOMODATIONS

Sanibel has many wonderful accomodation options. We have secured a wedding room block with the Sanibel Inn, which is where our ceremony will be taking place. To book the Sanibel Inn at the discounted wedding block rate please follow these instructions:

  • Click the image below to see the available room options and decide on your preferred room type.
  • Contact us (Greg or Lauren) by phone or email directly with your room type choice.
  • We will provide you with a hotel room confirmation number for your room type.
  • Once you have your confirmation number call the Inns of Sanibel at (239) 472-5170 Monday-Friday between 7am and 3pm EST and ask for Jason.
  • Provide your confirmation number and payment information to Jason to secure your room.

There are limited numbers of each room type, so to ensure you get your first choice please book early! Our discounted rate is 20% off of the normal Sanibel Inn prices. This discount is only available for rooms booked before the end of July of 2018.
